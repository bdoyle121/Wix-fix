About A Night to Nourish

A Night to Nourish is a community event hosted at Snipes Farm to raise awareness and support for hunger relief efforts in Lower Bucks County. It brings together local nonprofits, donors, and families for an evening of music, food, and giving.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is dinner included?
A: Yes! You can bring your own or choose from optional boxed dinners.

Q: Where is the event?
A: Snipes Farm, Bucks County, PA.

Q: Is this event kid-friendly?
A: Absolutely! Families are welcome.
